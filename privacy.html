Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 14, 2026

This application is an internal reporting tool developed to retrieve financial reports from QuickBooks Online for authorized users.

No personal information is sold, shared, or distributed to third parties.

The application only accesses QuickBooks Online data after explicit authorization by the QuickBooks account owner.

All retrieved information is used solely for internal financial reporting purposes.

No financial information is stored outside authorized Google services except where explicitly configured by the user.

If you have questions regarding this application, please contact the application owner.
